About Initiative for Sustainable Community Development (ISCoD Kenya)

The Initiative for Sustainable Community Development (ISCoD Kenya) is a legally registered local NGO under the Kenya NGO Coordination Board since 2021. Operating in Kisii, Nyamira, Migori, Homabay, Bomet, and Narok counties, ISCoD Kenya also extends its impact beyond these regions through strategic partnerships and collaborations.

vision is to foster thriving and resilient local communities. Guided by our mission, we empower communities to build resilience and achieve sustainable growth by addressing critical development priorities such as Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH), environmental sustainability, climate action, research and development, and the integration of technology into sustainable development initiatives.
